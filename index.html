Hack On The Hill

Hosted by UCLA ACM and Res Life

Hack On The Hill is a 12 hour, beginner-friendly hackathon.

A hackathon brings students together to build cool things. If you haven't been to one then don't worry, we've designed this hackathon for you.

Even if you've never written a line of code, we're happy to have you. All you need is the desire to learn something new.
